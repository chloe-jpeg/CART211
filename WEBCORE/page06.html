How is social media involved? - Siliconrepublic

A few of the Prime Spaces for targeted ads to appear is on social media, which many people spend a lot of their screentime on. It is the price to pay to continue using these free-ish platforms, unfortunately they are very good in the buying data game. Tiktok shops or sponsored Instagram posts that are getting more and more accurate are on everyone's feed.

"It is supposed to be a simple deal, users watch ads so platforms generate enough revenue to run and scale their sites. However, social media nowadays feels more like a shopping mall than a place to consume and share non-commercial content."
