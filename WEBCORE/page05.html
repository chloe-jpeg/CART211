Ads are buying us? - Usercentrics

Data is collected every time the internet is in use and is then being sold off to global companies, where they can analyse and information to make ads as targeted as possible.

"Data comes from nearly everywhere online, even when many people think they're not doing anything. Browsers, apps, and other software can record IP addresses, revealing location. They also log browser type and version, operating system, device type, and whether a paid or free version of an app is in use."

Overconsumption is a widespread modern issue, and how can it not be when ads are shoved in our face every paragraph, before, between, after videos, once every 5 short forms of content... To demarque themselves from everything sold, companies have to propose something that YOU are gonna be interested in. That's where your data comes in, what you like is of interest to global corporations.
